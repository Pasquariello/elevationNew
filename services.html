WHAT WE DO

Elevation Companies offers a wide variety of real estate related services. This far-reaching knowledge gives our industry professionals the expertise guaranteed to get the job done.

Development and Construction


Elevation Companies develops medical office buildings exclusively as a first-party owner and operator. We have completed two projects to date and expect to have the third ready for occupancy in the first quarter of 2020. All of our buildings are 100% owned by Elevation Companies and leased to the Department of Veteran Affairs. Our team is very proud of our partnership with the Department of Veterans Affairs and supporting the veterans in their local communities. Elevation Companies purchased the land, worked through the entitlement process with the respective city governments, designed the project and constructed the buildings through our subsidiary TM Squared. Integrating the development of a project with construction and ultimately property management creates a seamless process for our future GSA tenant. Tom Foster is very adept at not only understanding the requirements of the RLP, but also working with the local Contacting Officer to help them identify alternative, cost saving solutions.

Property Management


The property management division of Elevation Companies is led by Marc Holmes. Marc has well over fifteen years of experience ranging from personally managing buildings of less than 10,000 square feet to overseeing the property management operations of a medical office portfolio exceeding 2,000,000 square feet. One of the traits that separates Elevation Companies from other firms is our attention to the financial side of the assets. Marc is a Certified Public Accountant and acts as an asset manager as much as overseeing the property management staff and operations. The clients of Elevation Companies can be confident not only that their buildings are being well maintained, but that their financial investment is being carefully monitored as well. Elevation Companies is always mindful that a coordinated strategy between our property management and leasing groups will result in the best possible return on investment for our clients.

Leasing


Elevation Companies leases primarily medical office buildings throughout Colorado, from Greeley to Durango. We understand that leasing is much more than sticking a sign in the ground. From targeted e- mail blasts to direct solicitation of tenants, our marketing strategies are second to none. Marc Holmes oversees the leasing initiative for Elevation Companies and recognizes that attracting leads is only half the battle. Working through the art of the deal is what produces executed leases. Marc and his team understand the intricate balancing act between the right amount of tenant improvement dollars versus rental rate versus length of the lease to satisfy both the tenant and landlord and ultimately close the deal. The Elevation Companies team has decades of experience helping both landlords and tenants alike achieve the best lease possible.

Wireless Consulting


Elevation Wireless was born primarily as a real estate entity to service wireless communication companies in the western United States and has morphed into a full service, premier consulting firm in the wireless industry. Led by Tom Foster, Elevation Wireless strives to meet our client’s needs by providing site acquisition services, planning and zoning approvals and construction oversight. While Elevation Wireless works in all sectors of the wireless communications industry we have a specialized focus on small cells, right of way projects and Distributed Antenna Systems. Tom and his team have over a decade of experience in the wireless industry which translates into an unparalleled level of service to the major carriers. Our team approach to site acquisition work differs from the industry standards in that we leverage the best skill sets of our entire team for every project.
